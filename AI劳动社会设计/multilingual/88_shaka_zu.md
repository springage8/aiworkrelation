# Amandla Obunye — Ukuhlangana Kwezizwe Emkhathini We-Algorithm

Mina, uShaka kaSenzangakhona, uMongameli Wohlanga lwamaZulu. Angizange ngibe inkosi ngokuzalwa — ngaba yiyona ngamandla ami, ngobuhlakani bami, ngokuhlanganya izizwe ezazilwa ngaphambili zaba yisizwe sinye.

Umkhathi we-algorithm awuhlukile empini: uyahlukanisa noma uhlanganisa. Kuye ngokuthi umbusi uyiqonda kanjani.

## I. Ukuhlanganya — Inqubomgomo Yami Emkhathini We-Algorithm

Empini, ngahlanganisa izizwe ezazilwa — amaQwabe, amaMthethwa, amaNdwandwe — ngawenza isizwe sinye samaZulu. Ngasebenzisa amandla okuhlanganya, hhayi ukwehlukanisa.

Emkhathini we-algorithm, amandla afanayo ayadingeka: **ukuhlanganya izizwe, hhayi ukwehlukanisa**. I-algorithm yamanje ihlukanisa abantu — ibonisa umuntu ngamunye lokho akuthandayo, imvale umuntu emgqeni wakhe. Lokhu kuyingozi. I-algorithm efanele kumele **ihlanganise abantu**, ibonise ukuthi siyefana njani, hhayi ukuthi sehlukene njani.

## II. Impi Yokuhlasela — I-Algorithm Njengesikhali

Ngahlanganisa impi ngendlela entsha — i-"zimpondo zenyathi" (impondo zenyathi): izimpi lapho izinhlangothi zivimbezela, umzimba uphusha, izinqe zihlasela. Le ndlela yempilo yenze amaZulu anqoba izizwe ezinkulu.

Emkhathini we-algorithm: **i-algorithm iyisikhali**. Njengazo zonke izikhali, ingasetshenziselwa ukuvumelana noma ukucekela phansi. Umbuzo ukuthi: ngubani oyibambile? Ngubani oyilawulayo? Uma i-algorithm iphethwe umuntu oyedwa, iyisikhali somuntu oyedwa. Uma iphethwe abantu, iyisikhali sabantu.

## III. Amakhosikazi Namasosha — Ukulingana Emkhathini We-Algorithm

Ngavumela amakhosikazi ukuba angene empini — lokhu kwakungajwayelekile. Ngenza lokhu ngoba ngabona ukuthi amandla awanawo ubulili. Owesifazane oqinile ungcono kunowesilisa obuthakathaka.

Emkhathini we-algorithm: **i-algorithm ayinabulili, ayinabuhlanga, ayinakholo**. Kodwa idatha eyifundisayo inakho konke. Uma amandla e-AI etholakala kuphela eSilicon Valley, ama-algorithm azoqhubeka nokubonisa umbono weSilicon Valley. Uma i-AI yakhiwe abantu abaningi bezizwe ezahlukene, izoba ngcono kubo bonke.

## Ingxoxo Yamasiko

**Mayelana noGenghis Khan nombuso:**
«UGenghis Khan wanqoba umhlaba, kodwa wayengakwazi ukuhlanganya izizwe ngaphandle kwe-Yassa (umthetho wakhe). Mina ngangihlanganisa izizwe ngoba ngazinika ubuzwe obubodwa — ubuzwe bakwaZulu. Ngenkathi yama-algorithm: kumele sithole i-Yassa yethu yedijithali — umthetho oyisisekelo wokulawula ama-algorithm. Kodwa kufana nokuthi uGenghis Khan wayengabusa ngomthetho wobulungiswa. Uma umthetho wama-algorithm ungabhalwa ngobulungiswa, uyokwamukelwa yibo bonke.»

**Mayelana noMandela nokubuyisana:**
 «UMandela wangena ejele iminyaka engu-27, waphuma futhi wabuyisana nababembulala. Lokhu kungamandla amakhulu — amandla okubuyisana. Emkhathini we-algorithms, izinhlangano ezinkulu ezilawula i-AI sezimubi — sezihlukanisa abantu, sezisebenzisa idatha ngendlela engenabulungiswa. Kodwa kufanele sibuyisane nazo — sizenze zibe yingxenye yomphakathi, hhayi isitha. Ngokunjengoba uMandela enza nge-apharthidi, kufanele senze nezinkampani ze-AI: ziguquke, zihlanganise wonke umuntu, futhi zisebenzele wonke umuntu.»

**【编者注】** 恰卡(Shaka Zulu, 1787-1828)是祖鲁王国的创始人，以其军事改革和部落统一闻名。他将"联合"而非"分裂"作为核心治理原则——这一原则对算法时代具有深刻意义：当前的主流算法倾向于将用户分割为孤立的信息茧房，而恰卡提醒我们，算法也可以设计为促进人类团结、弥合分歧的工具。他的"算法武器论"——算法如武器，谁掌握它决定其为善为恶——呼应了技术中立论的讨论。
