# Sanarwar Ranar Ma'aikata: Tsarin Dangantakar Samar da Mutane a cikin Al'ummar Aikin AI

**Kwanan wata:** 1 ga Mayu, 2026 — Ranar Ma'aikatan Duniya
**Madogarar Ka'idoji:** Marx, Weber, Durkheim, Foucault, Bourdieu, Baudrillard, Žižek, Habermas, Ellul

---

## Bincike na Jigo: Babban Rikici na Zamanin AI

> *"Niƙar hannu yana ba ka al'umma da mai mulkin feudal; niƙar tururi yana ba ka al'umma da ɗan jari-hujja na masana'antu."* — Marx

Hankali na Wucin Gadi (AI) shine babban tsalle mafi girma a cikin ƙarfin samarwa a tarihin ɗan adam. Amma ƙarfin samarwa kaɗai ba ya kawo 'yanci kai tsaye — **dole ne dangantakar samarwa ma ta canza**.

**Babban rikicin zamaninmu:**
- **Ƙarfin samarwa ya zama na zamantakewa** — bayanai, algorithms, da ƙarfin lissafi sune samfurin gamayya na biliyoyin mutane
- **Dangantakar samarwa ta kasance masu zaman kansu** — waɗannan albarkatu suna ƙarƙashin mallakar wasu kamfanoni kaɗan
- **Sakamako:** AI, wanda zai iya 'yantar da ɗan adam daga ƙarancin abinci, a maimakon haka yana ƙara zurfafa rashin daidaito, sa ido, da nisantarwa

**Zaɓin da ke gabanmu:**
| **Hanyar A — AI Feudalism** | **Hanyar B — 'Yancin AI** |
|---|---|
| Kaɗan ne ke sarrafa algorithms, yawancin sun zama bayin bayanai | AI ya zama kayan aiki don 'yantar da dukan bil'adama |
| Yaƙi ya zama atomatik ta AI, albarkatu sun zama ƙananan ƙirƙira | An kawar da ƙarancin abinci, an ƙirƙiri tushen zaman lafiya |
| Gudanarwar algorithmic tana zurfafa dukkan nau'ikan nisantarwa guda huɗu | Lokacin hutu ya zama ma'aunin dukiya na gaske |

## Ƙa'idodi Guda Uku na Jigo

### Ƙa'ida 1: Bayanai a Matsayin Dukiyar Jama'a
| Bayanai masu zaman kansu | → | Haƙƙin mutum wanda ba za a iya ba da shi ba (BA haɗin kai ba) |
| Bayanai na zamantakewa | → | Amintacciyar jama'a (kamar ɗakin karatu na jama'a) |
| Algorithms masu mahimmanci | → | Kayayyakin jama'a masu buɗaɗɗen tushe |
| Kayayyakin lissafi | → | Amfanin jama'a (kamar ruwa da wutar lantarki) |

### Ƙa'ida 2: Dimokuradiyyar Algorithmic
- Dokokin da algorithms suka kafa → dole ne su kasance ƙarƙashin tattaunawa ta dimokuradiyya daga waɗanda abin ya shafa
- Abubuwan da ake buƙata: **Mai iya fahimta, Mai iya kalubalantar, Mai iya ba da lissafi**
- Manufofin ingantawa: ba kawai inganci ba, amma adalci, mutunci, da jin daɗi

### Ƙa'ida 3: Aiki na 'Yanci
- Lokacin aikin da ake buƙata: an rage shi zuwa kusan awanni 12–20 a kowane mako
- Aiki ya daina zama hanyar rayuwa kawai → ya zama "babban buƙatun rayuwa"
- Babban Kuɗin Samun Rayuwa na Duniya yana tabbatar da rayuwar abin duniya
- Lokacin hutu ya zama sarari don ci gaban ɗan adam cikakke

## Ginshiƙai Biyar na Tsarin Cibiyoyi

| Ginshiƙi | Matsalar da Aka Magance | Sabon Abin |
|----------|------------------------|------------|
| **Tsarin Rarraba Bayanai** | Mallakar dandamali yana kama 100% na ƙimar bayanai | Amintacciyar Bayanai ta Jama'a → tana rarraba 50% na ribar AI ga dukan mutane |
| **Bayyananniyar Algorithm da Bincike** | Algorithms akwatunan baƙi ne ba tare da halalci ba | Bincike mai matakai uku: na ciki → mai zaman kansa → na zamantakewa (shigar jama'a) |
| **Ƙungiyar Dijital** | Rushewar aiki, nisantarwa | Ƙungiyoyin haɗin gwiwar AI mallakar ma'aikata, ana gudanar da su ta dimokuradiyya |
| **Mulkin Lokaci** | Jari ya mallaki duk sa'o'in farkawa | Mafi girman mako na aiki na awa 20, hutu na kirkirar da aka biya, haƙƙoƙin "kashe ba a sa ido ba" |
| **Samar da AI da Aka Rarraba** | Ƙarfin AI ya tattara a hannun kaɗan | Grid lissafi na jama'a, tsarin samfuran buɗaɗɗen tushe, haɗin gwiwar AI na tarayya |

## Taswirar Hanyar Canjin Zaman Lafiya

### Mataki na 1: Dakatar da Zubar da Jini (2026–2028)
- Haramta makamai masu kashe kai na atomatik gaba ɗaya
- "Harajin yaƙi" na 50% kan ribar da ta wuce kima na 'yan kwangilar tsaro
- Bayyana yadda kuɗaɗen yaƙi ke aiki
- Kwamitocin da'a na AI tare da sharuɗɗan "babu amfani da soja"

### Mataki na 2: Canjin Cibiyoyi (2028–2035)
- Yarjejeniyar Algorithm ta Duniya ta fara aiki
- Canjin masana'antu daga soja zuwa farar hula (jagorar makami mai linzami → magani mai mahimmanci, jirage marasa matuƙi → taimakon bala'i)
- 50% na kasafin kuɗin soja an tura su zuwa maido da muhalli, Babban Kuɗin Samun Rayuwa na Duniya, ci gaban Kudancin Duniya
- Amintacciyar jama'a don makamashi, abinci, ruwa da lissafi

### Mataki na 3: Tsarin Zaman Lafiya (2035–2050)
- Rage kasafin kuɗin soja na duniya zuwa ƙasa da 1% na GDP
- An lalata makamai masu sarrafa kansu a duniya, haramta na dindindin
- An kawar da ƙarancin abinci ta hanyar sarrafa albarkatu da AI ya inganta
- Yaƙi ya zama tsohon zamani kamar fafatawa ta feudal

## Kira don Aiki

**Ga dukkan ma'aikata:** Ku shirya — ku shiga ko ƙirƙiri ƙungiyoyin algorithm / haɗin gwiwar dijital. Ku nemi bayyananniyar algorithm. Ku aiwatar da hanyoyin daban (kuyi amfani da AI mai buɗaɗɗen tushe, ku tallafa dandamalin haɗin gwiwa).

**Ga masu tsara manufofi:** Ku ba da fifiko ga doka — bayanai a matsayin albarkatun jama'a, kimanta tasirin AI na dole, ƙananan ƙa'idodin bayyananniyar algorithm. Ku saka hannun jari a kayayyakin lissafi na jama'a.

**Ga masu fasaha:** Tsarin da kuke ginawa zai shafi biliyoyin mutane. Shin samfurin ku yana 'yantar da mutane ne ko maye gurbinsu? Shin algorithm ɗin ku yana ƙarfafa ma'aikata ne ko kuma yana cire musu? **Zaɓuɓɓukan fasaha ba su da tsaka-tsaki.**

**Ga 'yan ƙasa na duniya:** Ku fallasa ƙarancin da aka ƙirƙira. Ku gina haɗin kai na ƙetaren ƙasa. Maƙiyi ba wata ƙasa ba ce — tsarin ne da ke cin riba daga rarrabuwa da yaƙi.

> *"Masana falsafa sun fassara duniya ta hanyoyi daban-daban kawai; mahimmin abu shine canza ta."* — Marx

A wannan Ranar Ma'aikata, mun sake tabbatarwa:
- Bari AI ya zama kayan aiki don 'yantar da aiki, ba makami don lalata shi ba
- Bari bayanai su zama dukiyar gama gari na dukan bil'adama, ba mallakar kaɗan ba
- Bari lokacin hutu ya zama ma'aunin dukiya na gaske

**Ba a hasashen makomai — an halicce ta.**
